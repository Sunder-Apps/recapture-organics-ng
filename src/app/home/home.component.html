What is the Entourage Effect?

The Entourage Effect is the multitude of added benefits one gets from utilizing a wide spectrum of cannabis plant components in unison, in comparison to the minimal benefits one would feel whilst taking advantage of only one component at a time — for example, a pure CBD Isolate product.
